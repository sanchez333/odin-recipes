Lasagna

Description

It is lasagna; noodle, sauce, cheese.

Very powerful.

Ingredients

  • Sauce
  • Noodle
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Noodle (Not too Much)
  2. Place Layer of Noodle in Baking Pan
  3. Place Layer of Sauce on Noodle
  4. Place Layer of Cheese on Sauce
  5. Repeat Steps 2-4 until Baking Pan is Full
  6. Bake at 734 Degrees Fahrenheit for 13 Hours
